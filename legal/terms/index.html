2. The Service

Cacheable is a charitable giving platform that:

  • Uses Plaid to read transaction data from a credit or debit card you designate, for the sole purpose of calculating round-up amounts
  • Calculates the difference between each transaction and the next whole dollar (your "round-ups")
  • Charges your designated payment method once per month for the accumulated round-up total (minimum $5.00)
  • Disburses the net amount quarterly to your chosen nonprofit via Endaoment, a registered 501(c)(3) donor-advised fund
  • Issues donation tax receipts through Endaoment

Cacheable is a technology platform, not a charity. Charitable deductions are facilitated through Endaoment, the 501(c)(3) that receives and disburses your donations.

Plaid's services are subject to Plaid's own terms and privacy policy. You can revoke Cacheable's access to your financial accounts at any time by visiting my.plaid.com or by removing the linked card in the app. Revoking access will stop future round-up calculations.

We access your transaction data solely to calculate round-up amounts. We do not use transaction data for any other purpose and do not sell it.

5. Fees and Charges

A platform fee is deducted from your monthly round-up total before disbursement to your chosen cause. The fee depends on your chosen payment method:

  • Bank account (ACH): 5% of your monthly round-up total
  • Apple Pay: 10% of your monthly round-up total
  • Credit or debit card: 10% of your monthly round-up total

These fees are deducted from your round-up total — you are charged only the round-up amount you accumulate. No additional fees are added to your payment.

If your accumulated round-ups are less than $5.00 in a given month, your balance rolls over to the following month and no charge is made until the $5.00 minimum is reached.

8. Donations, DAF Structure, and Tax Receipts

Cacheable routes donations through Endaoment, a registered 501(c)(3) donor-advised fund (EIN 84-4236469). By using Cacheable, you understand and agree to the following:

  • Donations are irrevocable. Once your monthly round-up total is submitted to Endaoment, it cannot be reversed, recalled, or refunded. This is a legal requirement of the donor-advised fund structure and is what qualifies your donation as tax-deductible. If you do not wish to donate a given month's round-ups, you must pause or cancel your account before the monthly charge date.

If your chosen nonprofit loses its charitable status, is dissolved, or becomes ineligible to receive grants under Endaoment's policies, Endaoment may redirect your donation to a similar cause at their discretion. Cacheable will notify you if we become aware of such a change and you will have the opportunity to select a new cause for future donations.

Changing your cause: You may change your designated cause at any time in Settings. Cause changes take effect the following day — all purchases made on the day you submit the change are still attributed to your previous cause. Starting the next calendar day, new round-ups go to your new cause. This means if you buy coffee at 11am and a sandwich at 1pm, then switch causes at 12pm, both purchases stay with your original cause; tomorrow's purchases go to the new one. Amounts already submitted to Endaoment in a prior quarter cannot be redirected.
