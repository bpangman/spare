Privacy Policy

PocketCache, Inc.

Effective Date: March 2026  |  Last Updated: March 2026

Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information PocketCache, Inc. ("PocketCache," "we," "us," or "our") collects, how we use it, who we share it with, and your rights regarding your information. Please read this carefully.

The short version: We collect only what we need to run the service. We never sell your personal information. Your bank credentials are handled by Plaid — we never see them. Your card data goes directly to Stripe — we never store it.

1. Information We Collect

A. Information You Provide Directly

  • Account information: Name, email address, and profile photo (from Apple, Google, or Facebook if you use those sign-in options, or entered directly)
  • Communications: Messages you send to our support team

B. Information From Third-Party Sign-In Providers

If you sign in using Apple, Google, or Facebook, we receive limited profile information from that provider consistent with your permissions:

ProviderWhat We May Receive
Apple Sign InName (optional), email address (or Apple-anonymized relay address)
GoogleName, email address, profile photo, language preference
Facebook LoginName, email address, profile photo, general location (city/region), age range, gender

We use the demographic information received from sign-in providers (age range, general location, giving preferences) in anonymized, aggregated form to help nonprofit partners understand donor trends. This data is never sold and is never shared in a way that identifies you individually.

C. Financial Information (via Third Parties)

  • Via Plaid: Bank account type, account balance (to verify sufficient funds), and transaction data (merchant name, amount, date) necessary to calculate round-ups. We do not receive or store your bank username, password, or full account number.
  • Via Stripe: We receive a payment token confirming your card is valid. We never receive or store your full card number, CVV, or expiration date.

D. Usage and Device Information

  • Device type, operating system, app version
  • IP address and general location (city/region level, derived from IP — not GPS)
  • App usage patterns: screens viewed, features used, time spent
  • Crash reports and performance data

E. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Your precise GPS location
  • Your bank login credentials
  • Your full card number, CVV, or PIN
  • Your Social Security Number
  • Information from users under 18

Nonprofit Partners

We share your name and donation totals with the nonprofit you choose, so they can acknowledge your contribution and issue tax receipts. We do not share your financial account details with nonprofits.

Anonymized Demographic Data

We share aggregated, anonymized demographic information (e.g., "35% of BGCA donors are in the 25–34 age range in the Midwest") with nonprofit partners to help them understand their donor base. This data cannot be used to identify you individually.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, subpoena, or other legal process, or if we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, protect your safety or the safety of others, investigate fraud, or comply with a government request.

Business Transfers

If PocketCache is acquired, merged, or sells substantially all of its assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you via email or in-app notice before your data is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

What We Do NOT Do

  • We do not sell your personal information to third parties.
  • We do not share your financial data with advertisers.
  • We do not use your transaction data to target you with ads.

4. Data Retention

We retain your account information and transaction history for as long as your account is active plus 7 years (to comply with financial recordkeeping requirements). After account closure, we delete or anonymize your personal information within 90 days, except where retention is required by law.

5. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • AES-256 encryption of data at rest
  • TLS 1.2+ encryption of data in transit
  • Multi-factor authentication support
  • Regular security audits
  • Limited employee access to personal data on a need-to-know basis

No system is 100% secure. In the event of a data breach that affects your rights, we will notify you within 72 hours of discovery, consistent with applicable law.

6. Children's Privacy

PocketCache is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If we learn we have collected information from a minor, we will delete it immediately. If you believe a minor has created an account, please contact us at privacy@pocketcache.app.

7. Your Rights & Choices

All Users

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: Update inaccurate information via app Settings or by emailing us
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and personal data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Opt out of marketing emails: Unsubscribe link in every marketing email, or toggle in app Settings
  • Withdraw demographic data consent: Toggle in app Settings → Privacy; withdrawal is prospective only
  • Unlink financial accounts: Any time via app Settings

California Residents (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act:

  • Right to Know: The categories and specific pieces of personal information collected about you
  • Right to Delete: Deletion of your personal information (subject to exceptions)
  • Right to Opt Out of Sale: We do not sell personal information, so this right is not applicable — but we honor it by policy
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your CCPA rights

To exercise your California rights, email privacy@pocketcache.app with "CCPA Request" in the subject line. We will respond within 45 days.

8. Cookies and Tracking

Our mobile app does not use browser cookies. We use Firebase Analytics and similar SDKs to collect anonymized usage data. You can opt out of analytics data collection in app Settings → Privacy → Analytics.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes via email or in-app notification at least 30 days before the effective date. Your continued use of PocketCache after the effective date constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
