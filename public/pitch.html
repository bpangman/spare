How It Works

Passive giving.
Powerful impact.

Donors set it up once. Every purchase they make rounds up to the nearest dollar — the difference goes straight to BGCA.

01

Sign up in seconds via Apple, Google, or Facebook. No forms, no friction.
02

03

Donate Passively
