4. How Round-Ups Work

When you link a bank account or payment card, PocketCache monitors your transactions through our third-party banking partner, Plaid Technologies, Inc. ("Plaid"). For each qualifying transaction, we calculate the difference between the transaction amount and the next whole dollar (the "round-up amount").

Round-up amounts accumulate in your PocketCache account and are disbursed to your chosen nonprofit organization on a quarterly basis (January 1, April 1, July 1, and October 1 of each year). We reserve the right to adjust the disbursement schedule with 30 days' notice.

Minimum disbursement: If your accumulated round-ups total less than $1.00 at the time of disbursement, the amount will roll over to the next quarter.

5. Processing Fee

PocketCache retains a 3% processing fee from each donation before disbursement to your chosen nonprofit. This fee covers platform operations, payment processing costs, compliance, and service maintenance. The remaining 97% of your donated round-ups is disbursed directly to your chosen nonprofit.

Example: If you accumulate $50.00 in round-ups during a quarter, PocketCache retains $1.50 (3%) and your chosen nonprofit receives $48.50.

This fee is disclosed at account setup and is non-negotiable. We may adjust the fee percentage with 60 days' prior notice to registered users. If you do not agree to a fee adjustment, you may close your account before the effective date.
